Ratatouille

Classic French Vegetable Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and herbs (thyme, basil)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© onion and garlic.
  2. Add bell pepper and cook until slightly soft.
  3. Add zucchini, eggplant, and tomatoes.
  4. Season with salt and herbs.
  5. Cover and simmer for 25-30 minutes.
