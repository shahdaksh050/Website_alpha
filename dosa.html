Masala Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter (fermented)
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add curry leaves, green chili, and sliced onion. Saut√© till translucent.
  3. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Set aside.
  4. Heat a non-stick tawa, spread dosa batter evenly to make a thin crepe.
  5. Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook till golden brown.
  6. Place the potato mixture in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
