Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potato, carrot, peas, cauliflower)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter
  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the vegetables and mash them.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes.
  3. Add pav bhaji masala and mashed vegetables, then cook for a few minutes.
  4. Toast pav in butter and serve with the bhaji, garnished with coriander.
