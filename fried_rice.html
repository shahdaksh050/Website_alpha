Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cold)
  • 1/2 cup carrots, beans, and capsicum (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic (chopped)
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil, add garlic and sauté till golden.
  2. Add vegetables, stir-fry on high flame for 2–3 mins.
  3. Add rice, soy sauce, pepper, and toss well.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
