Quesadilla

Ingredients:

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or beef (optional)
  • 1/4 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan and brush it with oil.
  2. Place one tortilla in the pan, sprinkle cheese, chicken, onions, and bell peppers on top.
  3. Top with another tortilla and cook until golden on both sides.
  4. Slice and serve with salsa or sour cream.
