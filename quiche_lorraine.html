Quiche Lorraine

French savory tart with cheese and filling

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 1 cup grated cheese (Gruyère preferred)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk or cream
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon or mushrooms (optional)
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line pie crust in a baking dish.
  2. Spread cheese and bacon/mushrooms evenly in crust.
  3. Whisk eggs with milk, season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  4. Pour the mixture into the crust.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and set.
