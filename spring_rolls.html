Veg Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup cabbage, carrots, and bell peppers (julienned)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornflour + water paste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Sauté vegetables on high flame for 2–3 mins, add sauces.
  2. Let the filling cool, then roll into wrappers.
  3. Seal edges with cornflour paste.
  4. Deep fry till golden and crispy.
