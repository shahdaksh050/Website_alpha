Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles, onion (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Form ground beef into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook on a hot skillet or grill for 3â€“4 minutes each side.
  3. Add cheese on top and let it melt.
  4. Toast the buns lightly.
  5. Assemble burger with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and sauces as desired.
  6. Serve hot with fries or coleslaw.
