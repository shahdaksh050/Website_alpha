Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strong coffee, cooled
  • 3 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1 pack ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow bowl, combine the coffee and coffee liqueur. Dip the ladyfingers quickly into the coffee mixture, then arrange them in a dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar together until pale and thick.
  3. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  4. Fold the mascarpone into the whipped cream mixture, then fold this into the egg mixture.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers in the dish.
  6. Layer with another set of dipped ladyfingers, followed by the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder and enjoy!
