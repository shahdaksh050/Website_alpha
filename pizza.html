Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sliced tomatoes or olives

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously.
  4. Add toppings like tomatoes or olives if using.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until the crust is crisp and cheese is melted.
  6. Top with basil leaves, drizzle olive oil, and sprinkle salt and pepper.
  7. Slice and serve hot!
