Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 French baguette or Italian loaf
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Slice the bread horizontally or into thick slices.
  3. In a bowl, mix butter, garlic, parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Spread the garlic butter generously over the bread.
  5. Top with grated parmesan if desired.
  6. Place on a baking tray and bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with pasta or soups!

Creamy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato puree, chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 5–6 minutes.
  4. Lower the heat and stir in the cream until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add cooked pasta and mix well to coat with the sauce.
  6. Top with fresh basil and cheese if desired. Serve hot!
