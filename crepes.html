French Crêpes

Delicate thin pancakes, sweet or savory

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk flour and eggs together, then add milk and water gradually.
  2. Mix in melted butter and a pinch of salt.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter and spread thin.
  4. Cook for 1-2 minutes per side until golden.
  5. Fill with fruits, chocolate, or savory toppings of choice.
