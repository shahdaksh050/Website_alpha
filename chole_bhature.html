Chole Bhature

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

For Bhature:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Boil the soaked chickpeas in a pressure cooker until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, then sauté onions till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for a minute, then add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas along with some water. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  6. For bhature, mix all dry ingredients and yogurt to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 2–3 hours.
  7. Roll into discs and deep fry till golden and puffed.
  8. Serve hot chole with bhature, onions, and pickle.
