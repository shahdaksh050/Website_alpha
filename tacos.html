Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Cook the meat with taco seasoning and onions.
  2. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  3. Assemble tacos with cooked meat, lettuce, cheese, tomato, and sour cream.
