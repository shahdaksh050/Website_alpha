Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 6 cups sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
  3. Place mixture into bottom crust. Cover with top crust, seal edges, and cut slits.
  4. Bake for 45–50 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Cool before serving with whipped cream or ice cream.
