Sushi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna), thinly sliced
  • Vegetables (avocado, cucumber), julienned
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then stir into the rice.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, spread rice evenly, and add fish and vegetables.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly and slice into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
