Hot and Sour Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups vegetable/chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, carrots, cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornflour (mixed in water)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring stock to a boil, add vegetables and sauces.
  2. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add cornflour slurry to thicken.
  4. Season and serve hot with spring onions.
