Delicious Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup black beans (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup guacamole (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a layer of tortilla chips on a baking sheet or oven-safe pan.
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the chips, ensuring the chips are well-covered.
  4. Add black beans, jalapeños, onions, and chopped tomatoes over the cheese (if using).
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the nachos are crispy.
  6. Once the nachos are out of the oven, top with sour cream, salsa, cilantro, and optional guacamole.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your cheesy nachos!
