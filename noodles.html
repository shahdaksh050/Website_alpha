Hakka Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 200g hakka noodles
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp green chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles as per instructions, drain and rinse with cold water. Toss with a little oil to avoid sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok, sauté garlic and onion for a minute.
  3. Add all veggies and stir fry on high flame for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add sauces, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Add boiled noodles and toss everything together on high heat for 2 more minutes.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Veg Manchurian

Ingredients for Balls:

  • 1 cup grated cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Ingredients for Sauce:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  • 2 tbsp spring onions
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour mixed with 1/4 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients for the balls. Form small balls and deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. For the sauce, heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic, ginger, and spring onions.
  3. Add sauces, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add cornflour slurry and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add fried balls and toss to coat evenly in the sauce.
  6. Garnish with spring onion greens and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
