Macarons

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Buttercream for filling

Instructions:

  1. Sift together powdered sugar and almond flour.
  2. Beat egg whites with cream of tartar until stiff peaks form. Gradually add granulated sugar.
  3. Fold in the almond mixture and food coloring until smooth.
  4. Pipe the mixture into circles on a baking sheet. Let it sit for 30 minutes to form a shell.
  5. Bake at 300°F (150°C) for 12-15 minutes. Let cool.
  6. Fill with buttercream and sandwich the macaron shells together.
