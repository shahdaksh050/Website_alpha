Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add onions and sauté till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes away.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, salt and cook till the oil separates.
  4. Add cream, garam masala and kasuri methi. Mix well.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5-6 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or jeera rice.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potato)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 2-3 cloves, 1 bay leaf, 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak basmati rice for 20 minutes, then cook and set aside.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Add whole spices and sauté onions till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, mixed vegetables, turmeric, and salt. Cook till veggies soften.
  4. Add curd and biryani masala. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  5. In a dish, layer rice and vegetable mix. Garnish with coriander and mint.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 10 minutes. Serve hot with raita.
