Nutella Strawberry Pancakes

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 10 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/4 cup Nutella
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, vanilla extract, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour a ladle of batter onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.
  6. Stack the pancakes and spread Nutella between each layer.
  7. Top with sliced strawberries and whipped cream.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy the deliciousness!
