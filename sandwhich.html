Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread (white or sourdough)
  • 2 tbsp butter (softened)
  • 4 slices cheddar or American cheese

Instructions:

  1. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place 2 slices, butter side down, on a skillet. Add cheese on top.
  3. Top with remaining slices, butter side up.
  4. Grill on medium heat for 2â€“3 minutes per side, until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. Cut diagonally and serve hot with tomato soup or ketchup.
